# Hi there, I'm Spyridon C. Katsoudas 👋

### 🚀 PhD Candidate | Computational Biomechanics | AI & Hemodynamics

I am an applied mathematician and computational researcher focusing on the intersection of **Fluid Dynamics**, **Machine Learning**, and **Cardiovascular Pathology**. My work aims to accelerate high-fidelity Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulations using Artificial Neural Networks to better understand complex blood flow patterns, thrombosis, and arterial stenosis.

---

### 🔬 Research Focus
* **Physics-Informed ML & Surrogates:** Developing fast, robust Deep Learning frameworks (MLPs, ANNs) to bypass computationally expensive RANS/SST k-ω simulations.
* **Topological Fluid Dynamics:** Applying advanced topological metrics (e.g., TSVI, LSA) to identify stagnation zones and platelet activation regions in cardiovascular flows.
* **Clinical Translation:** Bridging rigorous applied mathematics with 4D Flow MRI data and clinical observations to predict thrombogenic risk.

---

### 📄 Featured Publication
* **[Accelerating the Simulation of Pulsatile Turbulent Hemodynamics in Stenotic Arteries using Artificial Neural Networks]** - *Meccanica (Springer)*
  *Developed a Machine-Learning framework built on high-fidelity CFD data to obtain a robust numerical solution for 3D turbulent flow in asymmetric and curved stenotic arteries, significantly reducing computational cost.*
